# Praisidio: Pivoting from Traditional HR Analytics to an AI Platform
Sole designer · Mar 2023 – Mar 2025 · Team: CEO, CTO, 3 engineers

---

## The Problem

Praisidio's original product was a traditional employee retention tool. The market was moving toward AI fast. The product had no clear differentiation and the company was at risk of becoming irrelevant.

---

## Outcome

Activation from 10% to 90%. Enterprise customers grew 10x. Average user saves one week of work per month. G2 rating 4.9 out of 5.

---

## What I Did

**1. Led the company pivot**
I facilitated a week-long workshop that generated 200+ insights from user research and market signals. The outcome was a full pivot to a conversational AI product: ChatGPT for workforce data. HR teams could ask questions in plain language and get instant analytics. A prototype built after the workshop generated interest from 50+ potential clients within one week and secured new funding.

**2. Found product-market fit**
I talked to 100+ target users including CHROs, VPs of Operations, and Talent Acquisition Managers across 25+ interview sessions. The core finding: analytics was fragmented, manual, last-minute, and error-prone. I synthesized the research into 6 themes that shaped the MVP. The product reached product-market fit within one year.

**3. Drove AI adoption from 10% to 90%**
Most users dropped off after asking a few questions. They did not trust the AI. I ran three design interventions:

Progressive explanations: users could verify how the AI calculated answers on demand. Activation went from 10% to 30%. Engineer time spent explaining AI answers dropped by 80%.

Dynamic exclusion filters: users could adjust which employee groups were included in a query in real time. This resolved mismatches between the AI's defaults and users' internal definitions. Activation went from 30% to 60%.

Trainable AI framing: I reframed the product from "AI that is always right" to "AI you can train." I redesigned onboarding, added structured feedback loops, and let users define custom terms in natural language. Activation reached 90%. This also eliminated 40+ hours per week of manual engineering work.

**4. Scaled to enterprise**
Once adoption was stable, the focus shifted to enterprise readiness: permission controls, view-based dashboards, custom reporting, team collaboration, and org-wide settings. Enterprise customers grew 10x. User accounts expanded 4x within existing customers.
